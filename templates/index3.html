Learn About ISL Grammar

Word Order

ISL typically follows Subject-Object-Verb (SOV) order, unlike English's Subject-Verb-Object (SVO) structure.

Time Expressions

Time references come at the beginning of sentences in ISL, establishing when an action occurs.

Negation

Negation is usually expressed by adding 'NO' or specific negative signs, rather than auxiliary verbs like "don't".
